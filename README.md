# Dijital Sosyal Hak Platformu

## Mission
Our mission is to make social rights accessible to all individuals, particularly focusing on the disabled, elderly, and chronically ill. We strive to empower these communities by providing information and resources that facilitate their access to essential social rights.

## Vision
We envision a society where everyone is aware of their social rights and has the ability to benefit from them. Our goal is to cultivate an inclusive environment that promotes equality and access for all.
